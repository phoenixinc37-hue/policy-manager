## Summary
A web app for Achilles Athletics that lets coaches create at-home practice plans and lets athletes follow the workout and enter their results. Coaches should be able to see real live updates from what the athlete is actually doing.

## Why it matters
- Creates athlete accountability
- Gives coaches live visibility into compliance and performance
- Turns home practice from assumption into trackable execution

## Final goal
- A working athlete workout logging web app that coaches can use to assign at-home practice plans and monitor athlete completion/results in real time

## Valuable final product
- A usable live Achilles Athletics workout-tracking app where coaches assign plans, athletes log workouts/results, and coaches can see real-time updates
