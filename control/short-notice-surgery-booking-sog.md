# control/short-notice-surgery-booking-sog.md

## Project
- P* short notice surgery booking SOG
- Quadrant: QD

## Summary
Create an SOG for short-notice surgery booking at Rosslyn and RV so staff can clearly determine when a surgery estimate should be enrolled into the short-notice surgery program.

## Why it matters
- Helps stabilize ups and downs in the surgery schedule
- Gives staff a clear operating standard
- Creates a more economical option for clients with limited funds

## Final goal
- A clear short-notice surgery booking SOG that Rosslyn and RV staff can understand and use consistently

## Valuable final product
- A finished, usable SOG that tells staff when and how to place a surgery estimate into the short-notice surgery program at Rosslyn and RV

## What is known
- Type: SOG
- Clinics: Rosslyn and RV
- Staff need to know when a surgery estimate belongs in the short-notice surgery program
- The idea is meant to stabilize surgery-schedule fluctuations
- The idea is also meant to create a lower-cost option for clients with limited funds

## What is missing
- Exact enrollment criteria
- Which surgery types qualify or do not qualify
- Pricing/discount logic
- Approval authority
- Scheduling workflow
- Client communication standard
- How this affects estimate presentation and consent
- Whether the process is identical at Rosslyn and RV or clinic-specific

## Next likely actions
- Keep in QD until intake is fuller
- Gather enrollment rules and business logic
- Draft the SOG once criteria and workflow are clearer
