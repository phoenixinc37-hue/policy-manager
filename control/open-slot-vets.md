## Project
- P* Open Slot - Vets
- Quadrant: QD

## Summary
A significant app concept where veterinary clinics can enter short-notice open surgery slots and offer a meaningful discount to clients. The other side of the app is for clients looking for discounts who have short-notice availability.

## Why it matters
- Helps clinics fill open surgery capacity
- Creates a discounted option for flexible clients
- Reduces wasted schedule capacity
- Could become a scalable marketplace product

## Final goal
- A working app where vet clinics can post discounted short-notice surgery openings and qualified clients can find and claim those openings

## Valuable final product
- A usable two-sided app connecting vet clinics with short-notice surgery openings to clients willing to accept discounted care on short notice

## What is known
- Project name: Open Slot - Vets
- It is a significant project
- Clinic side: enter short-notice open slots for surgeries and offer significant discounts
- Client side: clients look for discounts and have short-notice availability
