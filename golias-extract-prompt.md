## EXTRACT FORMAT

### 1. PERSONAL INTRO
- Name, age, location
- What you do (career background)
- Family situation (kids, etc.)
- Current life status (separated, in process of divorce, etc.)

### 2. SUMMITS OVERVIEW (1 sentence each)
For each of your 7 business "Summits":
- What is it?
- What's the status? (active, hold, etc.)

### 3. DIVORCE CONTEXT
- Who is Tammy? (ex-wife situation)
- What are the key issues?
- Any immediate decisions pending?
- What support do you need?

### 4. IMMEDIATE PRIORITIES
- What's the #1 thing this week?
- What's stuck?
- What's pending a decision?

### 5. ANYTHING ELSE
- Goals (top 1% AI)
- Anything else I should know

---

## OUTPUT STYLE

Use the same format as the CSI extracts:
- Key sections with headers
- Bullet points for facts
- 1-line "bottom line" summary at the end

**Start your response with:**
"SCOTT PERSONAL EXTRACT — [date]"

**End with:**
"EXTRACT COMPLETE"
