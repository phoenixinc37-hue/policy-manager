## OVERVIEW (Page 1)
Collaborative law is interest-based — not position-based.
- **Position:** "I want the house" (one answer)
- **Interest:** "I need stable housing for the kids and financial security" (many possible solutions)

Goal: Identify what truly matters to you, not just what you want to "win."

---

## GOALS RELATING TO CHILDREN (Page 2)

**General wellbeing:**
- Children to be well-adjusted, happy, feel good about themselves

**Consistent parenting:**
- Consistent discipline, expectations, consequences in both homes
- Both parents support each other's parenting decisions

**Parenting time:**
- Meaningful contact with both parents
- Not away from children for more than ___ days at a time

**Decision making:**
- Participate in major decisions affecting children's lives

**Keeping kids free from conflict:**
- Children free from divorce conflict
- Children don't feel they have to report on one parent to the other
- Financial issues don't spill into parenting
- Children don't feel pressure to reject either parent
- Children comfortable talking about time at both homes
