# Website Builder Pricing Summary and Comparison

## Network Solutions / Web.com Style Website Builder

Based on the email offer and current public pricing, this company appears to be selling a **DIY website builder platform** rather than a true done-for-you website service.

### Typical pricing
- Basic website plan: approximately **$1.99/month introductory**
- Renewal pricing after intro period: approximately **$16.99/month**
- Website + marketing plan: approximately **$3.99/month introductory**
- eCommerce plan: approximately **$9.99/month introductory**
- eCommerce renewal pricing: approximately **$29.99/month**

### What is typically included
- DIY website builder
- domain
- hosting
- email
- security / SSL
- stock images

### Important limitation
The low prices appear to be **introductory teaser rates**, and the offer is mainly for **software access**. The customer is still expected to build, manage, and maintain the site themselves unless they purchase additional help.
