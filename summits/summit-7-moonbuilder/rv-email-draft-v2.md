**Context — Inflation Adjustment Methodology**

Canadian inflation has averaged approximately **3% per year** over the past several years. The adjusted prices in this report use that rate to realign services that have not been updated in 12 months or more.

The intent is simply to keep pace — not to push pricing beyond what the market has already absorbed.

That said, this also gives us the opportunity to evaluate whether certain services should be priced **above that baseline** based on value, demand, or complexity.

---

**Report Overview**

- **Old Price** — current price in the system
- **New Price** — adjusted price aligned with the 3% annual average
- **Delta / Change** — dollar and percentage increase
- **Usage/Year** — number of times the service was billed in 2025
- **Last Updated** — last time the price was changed in the system
- **Days Since** — number of days since the last increase

The report only includes services that have gone **more than 365 days without a price adjustment**.
