# Summit 5 — Save Sash Today
**Tagline:** Fill the Gap. Get the Deal.
**Status:** Concept / Research complete — platform not yet built

---

## Core Concept
A last-minute appointment marketplace. Businesses with unexpected vacancies offer discounted slots. Clients with flexible schedules get deals. Platform takes a transaction fee.

**The Expedia Model:** Pricing is confidential — clinic names not disclosed until booking confirmed. Two clients may pay different prices. Neither knows what the other paid.

**The Revenue Mechanic:**
- Client pays $25 non-refundable deposit to secure slot
- Business fills dead time at discounted rate
- Platform earns fee per transaction ($10-$25)

---

## The CAC Flip (Key Pitch)
Traditional patient acquisition cost (CAC) for medical clinics: $155-$287
With Save Sash Today: Clinic EARNS $25 deposit per new patient acquired
**Swing: $200-$300+ per new patient vs. spending that amount**

## How It Works

**For businesses:**
1. Enroll practice
2. Update availability when slots open (cancellations, no-shows)
3. System matches with available clients
4. Client attends, business gets paid

**For clients:**
1. Register with location and availability
2. Receive match notifications for discounted same-day slots
3. Pay $25 non-refundable deposit to confirm
4. Receive clinic details after booking confirmed
5. Attend appointment

**Client-facing ad copy (no clinic names):**
"Looking for affordable same-day appointments in your area? Discounted rates available today for clients who can attend on short notice. Clinic details provided upon booking."

---

## Key Insights from Research
- No dedicated service exists for this in vet clinics — gap confirmed
- Professional associations regulate members but NOT third-party platforms
- Confidential pricing protects clinic's standard rate integrity
- Deposit = commitment mechanism, reduces no-shows
- Works for dentists, medical, and most appointment-based businesses
- Internationally trained dentists / new practices = high-interest early adopters
