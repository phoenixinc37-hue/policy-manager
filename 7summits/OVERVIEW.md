# The 7 Summits — Master Overview

## Core Philosophy
Financial security through diversification across 7 distinct income streams. Each summit is independent, connected, scalable, and at a different stage of development. Goal: control, optionality, and legacy.

## The Ecosystem
Summits are nodes, not silos. Capital, knowledge, and opportunities flow between them:
- Summit 2 → funds Summit 3
- Summit 1 → validates Summit 7
- Summit 3 → fuels Summit 6 content
- Summit 4 → feeds Summit 6 storytelling
- Summit 5 → could integrate into Summit 7 (clinic gaps)
