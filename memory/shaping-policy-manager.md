# Shaping Policy Manager

## Current Phase: Human-Compatible UI Polish
Objective: 10 small changes defined in chat, then pushed live in one swoop.

### Planned Changes (6/10)
1. **Primary Colors:** Forest Green and Light Grey shading.
2. **App Logo:** Change the generic "PM" square icon next to the "Policy Manager" title to a **Filing Cabinet** icon.
3. **Company Names (Sidebar):** Keep "Business Inc" as text-only under the main title. Remove the generic "Accounting" / "Law Firm" demo label text beneath that, and instead insert the **Target Business Name** (the name of the company we are pitching to) into that space as dynamic text.
4. **Target Business Sub-Box:** Inside the same box under the Target Business Name, keep the industry label (e.g. "Accounting") and add two options: **Demo** and **Live**.
5. **Hero Section Copy (Mobile/Desktop Home):** Rewrite the main landing page text.
   - **Headline:** "The customized foundation for your firm's operations."
   - **Subtext:** Focus heavily on the real problem: the problem isn't coming up with the solution, the problem is deploying the information so everyone on the team actually knows what they need to know. 
6. **Hero CTA Buttons:** Change "Get Started" and "Learn More" on the homepage to **"View Demo"** and **"Start a Blank Workspace"**.

## Project Status
The app is currently a strong beta candidate ("clay"). It supports Accounting, Law, and Veterinary presets with dynamic terminology and seeded policies. It has persistence (localStorage) and an AI-assistant draft generator.
